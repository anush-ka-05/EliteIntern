Tribute to Shakuntala Devi

Shakuntla Devi, an Indian mathematician and mental calculator renowned for her remarkable ability to solve intricate mathematical problems mentally, earned the moniker "Human Computer."

Her extraordinary talent gained recognition at the age of six, as she demonstrated the ability to solve complex mathematical problems without relying on calculators, pen, or paper.

Breaking gender stereotypes in a predominantly male-dominated field, Shakuntala Devi paved the way for future generations of women in mathematics. Her life story serves as an inspiration for individuals aspiring to defy societal expectations and pursue their passions relentlessly.
